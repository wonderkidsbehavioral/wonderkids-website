Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

This policy explains how WonderKids Behavioral Services may collect, use and protect information submitted through this website.

Information we collect

We may collect contact information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, child age range, service interest and general message. We may also receive basic technical information such as browser type, device type and pages viewed through analytics tools.

How information is used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, verify service availability, assist with insurance questions, improve the website and comply with legal obligations.

Please do not submit sensitive medical information

The general website contact form is not intended for detailed diagnoses, treatment records, insurance cards or other protected health information. Secure intake methods will be provided when appropriate.

Sharing and retention

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that support website hosting, communications or business operations under appropriate agreements. Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary.

Contact

Questions may be sent to info@wonderkidsbehavioral.com or (951) 538-7451.
