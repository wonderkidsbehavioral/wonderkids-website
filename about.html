Get to know us

Compassionate Experts. Purposeful Care.

WonderKids Behavioral Services is built around one belief: every child deserves thoughtful, individualized support that helps them grow with confidence.

Built on experience

More than a decade in the ABA field.

Backed by over 10 years of experience in Applied Behavior Analysis, our team is passionate about helping children build meaningful skills that last a lifetime. Through individualized, evidence-based therapy and close collaboration with families, we support communication, social interaction, independence and everyday participation.

We understand that progress is rarely one big moment. It is built through many small victories—asking for help, trying something new, completing a routine or connecting with another person. We celebrate each one.
