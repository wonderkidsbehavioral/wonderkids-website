Our mission

Meaningful progress that lasts beyond the therapy session.

Our mission is to help children discover their strengths, build essential skills and participate more fully in family, school and community life. We combine evidence-based ABA with warmth, creativity and close family partnership.

  • Individualized goals based on each child’s needs
  • Clear progress monitoring and family communication
  • Respectful, strengths-based and child-centered care
  • Strategies that carry over into everyday routines
