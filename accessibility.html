Accessibility Statement

Accessibility Statement

WonderKids Behavioral Services is committed to providing a website that is accessible to people with disabilities.

Our approach

We aim to use clear headings, readable contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation, descriptive alternative text and responsive layouts. We continue to review the website as content and technology change.

Need assistance?

If you experience difficulty using this website or need information in another format, contact us at (909)206-6865 or info@wonderkidsbehavioral.com. Please describe the page and the assistance needed.

Ongoing improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We welcome feedback and will make reasonable efforts to address identified barriers.
